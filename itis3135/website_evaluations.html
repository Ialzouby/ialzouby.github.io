Website Evaluations

Apple

An example of a good website!

This is Apple's main website! It allows you to get anywhere from sales to being able to log into your iCloud account!

  • Contrast: Apple chooses a consistent color scheme that does not have negative effects on the viewability of the text or elements around.
  • Repetition: The colors chosen are consistent and repeat throughout the website in the proper locations.
  • Alignment: I have never seen anything out of line on Apple's website, and I visit it frequently.
  • Proximity: It has good proximity as everything is in the proper area with relation to other functions in that area.

Yale School of Art

An example of an awful website!

This appears to be the website detailing all of Yale's art events. It allows you to login to your own account and see events and more!

  • Contrast: The colors are ugly and make the website look bad, however it is not hard to read or see any elements.
  • Repetition: The website has almost every color of the rainbow. It does not use enough repetition.
  • Alignment: Many things are off-centered and unaligned, there is too much empty space to the right.
  • Proximity: The tabs are on the left side, the login menu is in a weird corner, the news block is in a random spot on the screen. Horrible proximity, you have to hunt for things!
